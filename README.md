# AI Impact on Jobs 2030 – Workforce Risk & Resilience Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze how Artificial Intelligence is expected to impact jobs by 2030, 
focusing on automation risk, skill demand, salary trends, and workforce resilience.

# Purpose
The AI Impact on Jobs 2030 dashboard provides a comprehensive, data-driven view of how AI adoption influences job roles across industries.
It enables users to explore automation probability, AI exposure, skill requirements, education levels, experience, 
and technology growth to understand future workforce trends and risks.

# Data Sourse
The dataset contains simulated and structured job-market data representing potential AI impact scenarios by 2030.
- **Source** – Kaggle 

# Tech Stack
- **Power BI Desktop** – Primary data visualization and dashboard development platform  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and unpivoting of skill-related columns  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures, KPIs, and conditional logic  
- **Data Modeling** – Relationships between job roles, skills, experience, and risk categories  
- **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview and sharing
